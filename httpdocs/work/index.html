Area guides in Selangor, Malaysia
Selangor is one of the thirteen states in Malaysia located on the west coast of Peninsular Malaysia where surrounded the federal territories of Kuala Lumpur, the capital of Malaysia and Putrajaya. The state capital is Shah Alam and royal capital is Klang. Selangor, together with Klang Valley, is the biggest city in Malaysia with the largest population of nearly 6,012.6 million (2014) and most developed states due to the modernization and industrialization in the Klang Valley as it is the largest economic contributor to the nation in term of Gross Domestic Produt (GDP) making up approximately 23% of total GDP Malaysia. With the higher standard of living compare to other states, Selangor is recorded the lowest poverty rate in the country.
Based on the population of ethnic groups in Selangor , Malay comprise more than half of the population at roughly 53% , followed by the Chinese , Indian and others. English is widely used and spoken within these areas as substantial amount of expatriates, skilled workforce, international investors are attracted to contribute to the state’s rapid development due to its industrial hub. Being one of the most developed states in the nation, local and international cuisine can be found easily everywhere without any hassle.
The major contributors to the economy of Selangor can be categorized to business, industry and service sector where it accounts almost 58% of the state’s total GDP. National car Proton and Perodua are produced and assembled in the industrial site of Selangor along with other well known imported vehicles such as Toyota, Nissan Volkswagen, Hyundi, Peugeot and many more are assembled in the state as well. It plays a key factor to drive the industrial development of Selangor and Klang Valley area. On the other hands, Agriculture is also a prospering sector of Selangor’s economy involving in the cultivation and growth of tropical fruits for instance star fruits, papayas and bananas.
Selangor offers a wide range of fascinating and scenic destination to tourist ranging from tropical rainforest, rural villages to urban shopping malls. Being the most developed states in the nation, Selangor infrastructure and communication facilities are among the best along with great educational for higher learning and health institutions.
The state is a major development area with good infrastructure such as expressway and public transport, places of interest are easily accessible with public transportation such as public buses, taxi, KTM Komuter railway and Light Rail Transit (LRT) network which serves 80% of the major area and extension of the network undergoing currently is to be covered the entire Selangor expected to complete by 2017 will radically improve and transform the public transport system. Furthermore, a broad network of expressway is built throughout the entire states which connect the south and the north.
Compare to other states, Selangor gather around 80% of all the resources as the central of Malaysia with well-developed commercial building include high rise office towers, business suits, shopping malls and various type of residential properties which has seen a robust investment in property aspect from local and international investors with huge and promising investment opportunity in Selangor.
