About Twinz Residences

Ms. Jun, Marketing Department
Q: What is the concept that you use for Twinz Residences?

A: We use the concept for creating the oasis in puchong area that offering a blend of modern living and the serenity of nature. This concept is inspired by the magnificence of nature. We are confident to make Twinz Residences in Puchong Jaya to be a symbol of modern living in a bustling township

Q: What are the features that Twinz Residences will provide?

A: We provide many features in Twinz Residences. Twinz Residences offers 278 tastefully designed homes with 5 design options namely Type A, B, C, C1 and D with built-up sizes ranging from 871 sq ft to 1,226 sq ft.

Each homes is fitted with porcelain tiles in the bedrooms, living room, dining and kitchen as well as ceramic tiles in the bathrooms. The homes are equipped with fire-rated timber doors and quality locksets as well as sanitary ware. The modern and functional living spaces feature comprehensive electrical fittings that include lighting, plug, fan, heater and air-conditioning points. Cable television points and fiber wall sockets are also readily available. A

Q: For the location, what makes Twinz Residences special?

A: Twinz Residences is positioned at a strategic vantage point, Twinz Residences offers a panoramic view of Puchong and neighbouring Bandar Sunway.

Residents get to enjoy diverse views of the pool, the riverfront and the neighbouring townships. Built to capitalise on the beauty of nature, the homes feature large glass panel windows that allow ample sunlight, excellent ventilation and a good view of the surrounding greenery.

Q: What are the popular amenities which is near and conveniences to reach from Twinz Residences?

A: Twinz Residences is strategically located in the heart of the Klang Valley which is near to the city destination. Twinz Residences has a world of conveniences and amenities at its doorstep. Popular hotspots nearby include IOI Mall, Giant Bandar Puteri, Tesco Puchong, Sunway Pyramid, Setia Walk and One City Mall. The commercial hubs of Puchong Jaya, Pusat Bandar Puchong and the Puchong Financial Corporate Centre in Bandar Puteri are within a stone’s throw from Twinz Residences presenting a wide range of business activities, restaurants, stores, banks and other retail outlets. Premier hospitals near to the development include Columbia Asia Hospital, Sunway Medical Centre and KPMC Puchong.

Puchong has a good selection of national and international schools within around 5km radius that include SK Puchong Jaya 2, SMK Puchong Jaya (A), SK Puchong Jaya 1, SMK Batu Lapan, SJKC Yak Chee and Taylor’s International School Puchong. Institutions of higher learning in the vicinity and within a convenient distance include Taylor’s Lakeside University College, Binary University of Management, Monash University and Spectrum International College of Technology.

The Ampang LRT line is scheduled to extend up to IOI Mall whilst the Sunway Line BRT station will be located at nearby Sunway Medical Centre. Twinz Residences is served by a host of highway networks that make commuting convenient, namely, the main nerve of Jalan Puchong, the Federal Highway, the Damansara-Puchong highway (LDP), the Shah Alam Expressway (KESAS), the New Pantai Expressway (NPE) and the ELITE highway.

The Kuala Lumpur International Airport (KLIA) is only about 50km away, whilst Malaysia’s silicon valley Cyberjaya is about 28km away. The federal administrative capital of Putrajaya is just 30km away..

About Floor Plan

Q: Can you describe the floor plan for each type of houses in Twinz Residences?

A: There are 5 type of houses in Twinz residences which are type A, B, C, C1 and D. Type A and B feature 3 bedrooms and 2 bathrooms with a utility yard. Type C and C1 comprise 3 bedrooms, 2 bathrooms, a utility room and a spacious lanai. Type D features 3 bedrooms, 2 bathrooms, a study room, a utility yard and 2 balconies.
