About Twinz Residences

Mr. Yau Wen Soon, Managing Director

We did the interview with Mr. Yau Wen Soon which is the Managing Director of the YGS property development on 9th October 2015 at 4:00pm.

Ariel View
Q: What is the concept that you use for this property project?

A: Twinz Residences are design in mind of uplift the new urban lifestyle in tandem with sustainable principles by providing sufficient social interaction spaces and promoting green features within the high-rise living. Its oasis of life is set to sprout out amidst the bustling cityscape of Puchong offering a blend of modern living and the serenity of nature.

Q: What are the speciality for each house/unit that this property will provide?

A: Twinz Residences offers 278 tastefully designed homes with 5 design options namely Type A, B, C, C1 and D with built-up sizes ranging from 871 sq ft to 1,226 sq ft. Every floor only comprises of 6 to 7 units apartment served by 3 exclusive lift. Every unit has a balcony with minimum 3 bedrooms and 2 bathrooms. It is fitted with porcelain tiles in the bedrooms, living room, dining and kitchen as well as ceramic tiles in the bathrooms. The homes are equipped with fire-rated timber doors and quality locksets as well as sanitary ware. The modern and functional living spaces feature comprehensive electrical fittings that include lighting, plug, fan, heater and air-conditioning points. Cable television points and fiber wall socket are also readily available.

Q: For the location, what makes this property special?

A: Twinz Residences is strategically located in Puchong Jaya which is in the heart of the Klang Valley. It is just a stone’s throw away from major highways, linking the community to superior destinations. It is positioned at a strategic vantage point that offers a panoramic view of Puchong and neighbouring Bandar Sunway. Residents get to enjoy diverse views of the pool, the riverfront and the neighbouring townships. Built to capitalise on the beauty of nature, the homes feature large glass panel windows that allow ample sunlight, excellent ventilation and a good view of the surrounding greenery.

Q: What are the popular amenities which is near and conveniences to reach from this property?

A: Twinz Residences has a world of conveniences and amenities at its doorstep. Popular hotspots nearby include IOI Mall, Giant Bandar Puteri, Tesco Puchong, Sunway Pyramid, Setia Walk and One City Mall. The commercial hubs of Puchong Jaya, Pusat Bandar Puchong and the Puchong Financial Corporate Centre in Bandar Puteri are within a stone’s throw from Twinz Residences presenting a wide range of business activities, restaurants, stores, banks and other retail outlets. Premier hospitals near to the development include Columbia Asia Hospital, Sunway Medical Centre and KPMC Puchong.
Puchong has a good selection of national and international schools within around 5km radius that include SK Puchong Jaya 2, SMK Puchong Jaya (A), SK Puchong Jaya 1, SMK Batu Lapan, SJKC Yak Chee and Taylor’s International School Puchong. Institutions of higher learning in the vicinity and within a convenient distance include Taylor’s Lakeside University College, Binary University of Management, Monash University and Spectrum International College of Technology.
The Ampang LRT line is scheduled to extend up to IOI Mall whilst the Sunway Line BRT station will be located at nearby Sunway Medical Centre. Twinz Residences is served by a host of highway networks that make commuting convenient, namely, the main nerve of Jalan Puchong, the Federal Highway, the Damansara-Puchong highway (LDP), the Shah Alam Expressway (KESAS), the New Pantai Expressway (NPE) and the ELITE highway.
The Kuala Lumpur International Airport (KLIA) is about 50km away, whilst Malaysia’s silicon valley Cyberjaya is only about 28km away. The federal administrative capital of Putrajaya is just 30km away.

Q: Can you describe the floor plan for each type of houses/unit in property?

A: All typical unit layouts are designed more towards family concept, in which all types are practically designed with minimum 3 bedrooms and 2 bathrooms. Type A 871sf and Type B 1027sf has 3 bedrooms, next larger unit like Type C 1181 and C1 1156sf has 3 bedrooms plus 1 utility or maids room, and the largest Type D 1226sf has 3 bedrooms and a study room. Most importantly a spacious balcony is provided for all units. In order to enhance community living environment and security to the buyer, no mixture of commercial element allowed in this development.

Q: What are the facilities which will available and can be used by the resident in this property?

A: Twinz Residences presents a fascinating array of recreational facilities that bring new life to modern living standards. Starting with the entrance of the development, Twinz Residences sports a grand entrance guarded by security personnel leading up to a welcoming green inspired lobby. Residents enjoy easy access to parking bays via an optimized ramp design. The play hut is an interesting area to pick the interest of kids, whilst the childcare centre offers reliable services to parents. The awesome crystalline pool with blue waters is indeed a sight to behold whilst the splash park provides an energetic recreational element. Other facilities include meeting rooms, male and female common washrooms, a general store, a cafeteria and a multipurpose hall. The forest walk brings man and nature to a common platform, whilst the lotus pod adds a touch of fairy tale mysticism. The gourmet kitchen is an innovative space to instill strong community spirit, whilst the barbecue lawn is an excellent venue to catch up with friends and family over good food. Work out the extra calories at the fully-equipped gymnasium and fitness lounge or just unwind after a hectic day in the soothing waters of the Jacuzzi. Spend an evening waltzing to the tune of the Blue Danube or thumping to the beat of rock music at the dance pod. The yoga deck is an ideal place to relax one’s mind and de-stress whilst the splash park offers an exciting zone of fun-filled water activities. Take in the sights and sounds of Puchong or enjoy a scenic view of the KL city skyline from the comforts of the Sky Park decorated with lush greenery and elegant landscaping. The development also sports male and female changing rooms and prayer rooms. The 3-tiered security system features smart card access, trained security personnel, CCTV surveillance and secure perimeter fencing.

Message from Developers

Q: Is there any message that you would like to tell to the people about this property?

A:
The Tower of “Twinz” are not conceived as autonomous objects, but defined by the spaces they create around them. The design of Twinz have created & evoke a large open space between the 2 towers, where the area are articulated into a lifestyle resorts pool & landscape garden environment. This area is called “Leisure Zone” act as connector between the two identical residential towers that reflect the name of Twinz.

To differentiate Twinz from others, we have created a unique and grand Lotus Pod within the Leisure Zone on the podium facilities at Level 4. The entire Leisure Zone focus on tranquility landscape and water concept with the grand Lotus Pod being the focal point provides an active sports zone for young teenagers. The active sports zone is surrounding by a series of recreational and functional space, like Jacuzzi Pool, Crystalline Infinity Pool, Splash Park, Play Hut, Gourmet Kitchen, Yoga Deck, etc.
Rain Water Harvesting Tank also provided for ground floor landscape irrigation and reduces domestic potable water consumption. The collection tank is located at Level 3 car-park floor, designated to harvest the rain water collected from upper floor opened roof space and store it for the irrigation usage via gravity feed later.

Every floor has 6 to 7 units and all units layout are incorporated with large opening to enhance cross ventilation. Common areas like corridors are naturally ventilated and the building orientation is optimized to prevailing sun and wind while not sacrificing the construction effectiveness and best practices. These features will definitely help to reduce the electricity consumption in long run.

The 3-tiered security system features smart card access, trained security personnel, CCTV surveillance, and secured perimeter fencing. Concept of cluster community living planning, TWINZ units are planned in 6 to 7 units per floor at each tower also encourage community bonding and enhance communal security and sense of security. It is the lowest number of units every floor compared to other similar developments in the vicinity.

We strongly believe that by incorporating the sustainable design features/approaches and provide sustainable lifestyle facilities will surely enhance house buyers’ satisfaction and happiness in long run which are key fundamentals for future house value appreciation.
